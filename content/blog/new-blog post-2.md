One of the best ways to get to know a place is to start your day where the locals do. On **Oregon’s Adventure Coast: Coos Bay, North Bend, Charleston**, [locally owned coffee shops and bakeries](https://www.oregonsadventurecoast.com/img/Dining-Coffee-Shop-Guide-08-25.pdf) aren’t just places to grab a quick latte or a pastry, but they’re gathering places where you can experience the character and everyday rhythm of our region.

To help you experience Oregon’s Adventure Coast like a local, we’ve gathered a list of well-loved neighborhood spots for great coffee and freshly baked treats. Whether you’re fueling up for a day of fishing, crabbing, beach walks, or state park exploring, or looking for a laid-back spot to pause mid-day and unwind, these locally owned coffeehouses and bakeries capture the spirit of Oregon’s Adventure Coast.

![A white ceramic cup of latte with heart-shaped foam art sits on a saucer beside a flaky croissant on a wooden board, with another coffee cup and pastries softly blurred in the background at a café table.](/img/coffee-shops-and-bakeries-oregon-coast.jpg)

## Top Coffeeshops & Bakeries for Visitors in **Coos Bay**

[**Empire Bakery**](https://www.facebook.com/p/Empire-Bakery-100057679507311/) - 575 Newmark Avenue, Coos Bay<br>A tiny bakery behind the Tokyo Bistro restaurant, featuring freshly baked sourdough loaves, ciabattas, focaccias, cookies, danishes, cinnamon rolls, and more. Their focus on traditional baking methods, simple ingredients, and limited daily production has made it a favorite among regulars who appreciate quality over quantity.<br>**What People Say:** *The chocolate croissant is the best I have ever had. Absolutely delicious.*<br><br>[**Empire Donuts**](https://www.facebook.com/CBEmpireDonuts/) - 114 N. Wasson St., Coos Bay<br>Visitors are raving about this sweet, family-owned and operated donut shop in the Historic Empire District. Swing by to try out a variety of fresh donuts daily, as well as great local coffee and other beverages. www.facebook.com/CBEmpireDonuts<br>**What People Say:** *Best experience and donuts in town.*<br><br>[**Espresso Mill & Bakery**](http://www.espressomill.com/)\- 520 S. Empire Blvd. Coos Bay<br>This delightful establishment, known for its delicious coffee, is a great place to stop before heading out to see the State Parks on Cape Arago Highway. Not only does it features breakfast sandwiches, wraps, pastries, and top-notch service - it also has a convenient drive-thru!<br>**What People Say:** *We make it a point to stop here every time we are passing through town with the delicious menu of food and beverages offered...The hospitality of the workers truly makes it a must-stop-spot for us.*<br><br>[**Kaffe 101**](http://order.toasttab.com/online/kaffe-101-171-s-broadway) - 171 S Broadway, Coos Bay<br>Cozy coffeehouse with a welcoming vibe in downtown Coos Bay; features a fireplace, classic espresso drinks, and breakfast & lunch options. A good stop when you want something familiar, done well, and easy to grab on your way through town.<br>**What people say:** *This is a beautiful cozy place to relax on your next trip to Coos Bay area! Plus a great selection of books for your reading pleasure.*<br><br>[**So It Goes Coffeehouse**](http://www.soitgoescoffee.com/) - 190 Central Ave, Coos Bay,<br>A favorite with locals, this neighborhood gem describes itself as an "Old school coffeehouse featuring killer coffee, full bakery, comfort food, art showings, live music, bad poetry readings, used books and music for sale, & occasional small absurdist theatrics." Visit their [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/soitgoescoffeehouse) for live music and a special event schedule.<br>**What people say:** *Loved this coffee place. Delicious lattes. House made bagels. Breakfast sandwich with fresh ingredients. Friendliest people.*

### Top Coffeeshops & Bakeries for Visitors in **North Bend**

<br>[**350 Degrees**](https://350degreesbakeshop.square.site/)\- 1964 Sherman Ave, North Bend<br>A small, independent bakery and coffee shop known for its welcoming feel in downtown North Bend, serving house-baked pastries, sweets, breakfast and lunch sandwiches, and coffee drinks in a casual setting. It’s an easy stop for a morning coffee and pastry, a midday treat, or a light lunch while exploring town.<br>**What People Say:** *Such an amazing and cool little bakery...These are some of the best scones I have had.*<br><br>[**​​Black Anchor Coffee House**](https://blackanchorcoffeehouse.com/)\- 3077 Broadway Ave, North Bend<br>Opened spring 2024, this neighborhood coffee shop and café has quickly become a local favorite, with patrons saying it feels “like more than just a coffee shop — it’s a place that makes you feel welcome and appreciated.” Whether you have time to linger or need a quick pick-me-up, they’ve got you covered with a convenient drive-through.<br>**What People Say:** *The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, and it’s become one of my favorite spots to relax, catch up with friends, or just take a quiet moment for myself.*

[**Coastal Coffee**](https://www.instagram.com/coastalcoffeecoos/) - 1875 Sherman Ave, North Bend<br>A well-loved coffee stop located inside Books by the Bay known for its great coffee, friendly service, and relaxed setting. They proudly serve Cafeto Organic Coffee and offer dine-in, takeout, drive-thru, and delivery options, making it easy to fit any schedule. In the heart of downtown North Bend, it’s a convenient choice for visitors heading out early for sightseeing or short on time.<br>**What People Say:** *Cool vibe, coffee bar nestled in a vintage local bookstore.*<br><br>[**Ezekiel's Tin Rooster**](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=61555631165245) - 2082 Sherman Avenue, North Bend<br>This eclectic coffee shop and pastry restaurant features savory croissants, freshly baked goods, house-roasted coffees, espresso, teas, and more! Just look for the rooster outside (it's a signal that they are open for business inside). <br>**What People Say:** *If you’re looking for a cozy, inviting spot to relax, chat, or get some work done, this local coffee shop is perfect!*

### <br>Top Coffeeshops & Bakeries for Visitors in Charleston<br>

[**Cape Arago Café & Espresso**](https://www.facebook.com/CapeAragoCafeNEspresso/)\- 91120 Cape Arago Hwy, Charleston<br>This cozy, community staple offers both sit-down dining and convenient drive-thru service right off of Cape Arago Highway. Breakfast served all hours with 10 different omelets, pancakes and French toast from the griddle, and Cape Arago Café's famous 4-pound breakfast burrito. Enjoy their burgers, sandwiches, and creative lunch specials. Drive-thru espresso. Call ahead for a meal and coffee pickup at the window.<br>**What people say:** *The café exudes cleanliness as well as friendliness that enhance the beachy vibe one hopes for. Don’t miss this place!*

[**Ladybread Bakery**](https://theladybreadbakery.com/) - 63345 Boat Basin Rd. Charleston<br>This well-loved artisan bakery focuses on freshly baked goods made with quality ingredients and time-tested techniques. You’ll find a rotating mix of breads and pastries, including cinnamon rolls, cookies, muffins, and seasonal specialty loaves. The space itself is simple and welcoming, doubling as a spot to linger while checking out local art and rotating displays. <br>**What People Say:** *Make the effort and visit this gem. Lovely owner and staff!* <br><br>[**Persephone Cafe & Bakery**](https://www.persephonecafeandbakery.com/) - 91152 Cape Arago Highway, Charleston<br>A combined café and bakery known for from-scratch baking with mostly organic ingredients and a welcoming setting that encourages conversation and connection. Offerings include freshly baked artisan breads, such as sourdough and seeded spelt, as well as muffins and cookies. The bakery grows its own herbs and sources much of its grain from Oregon mills, reflecting a thoughtful approach to regional food traditions. <br>**What People Say:** *This was a wonderful experience! Bobak, the artisan baker, infuses the bread and atmosphere with his passion for baking. The quality and flavor is evident in every delicious bite.*
